VeniVidi

Venividi is a virtual guide application leading the user through the most interesting places and monuments of the cities he visits and at the same time enabling him to compete against his friends using gamification. It lets you experience the new way of sightseeing!

Loco

Loco is a simple and intuitive communicator for local communities. The application enables users to contact their friends and meet new people from their neighbourhood or promote their businesses and services by posting short advertisements.
